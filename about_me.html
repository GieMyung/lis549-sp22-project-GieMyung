GieMyung's Biography

GieMyung was born and raised in South Korea. GieMyung came to the United States at age of 17 and graduated Indiana University Bloomington with Informatics degree. He was very passionate about learning and he decided to continue his study at the University of Washington studying Information Management.

GieMyung's first major was not Informatics. He had a dream to become a successful businessman just like his father. Wandering without feeling the joy of learning, GieMyung found his interest when he first took Python class. He then changed his major to Informatics to learn deeper.

GieMyung has gained experience as a front-end developer based on his acadmemic knowledge and now he is building more professional knowledge at the University of Washington. His passion and curiosity are his greatest strengths. Especially, he has an interest in software.
